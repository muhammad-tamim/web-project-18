Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?
You can begin by choosing your current level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced) and then follow the lessons, practice exercises, and quizzes available in each section.
Do I need to know any English before starting?
No. Our lessons are designed for complete beginners as well as learners who already know some English. You can select your level and learn step by step.
How can I improve my speaking skills?
Practice regularly with our speaking exercises, record your voice, and compare it with native speakers. You can also join live conversation sessions or practice with friends using our speaking practice activities.
Are there any free resources available?
Yes! We offer free lessons, grammar guides, vocabulary lists, and practice quizzes. Premium members get access to advanced lessons, live classes, and personalized feedback.
How much time should I study every day?
Even 15-30 minutes a day can help you make progress. The key is consistency— study a little every day instead of long sessions once in a while.
